The Ancient Pagodas of Myanmar

Bagan is an ancient city and a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Myanmar.
During the 11th and 13th centuries, more than 10,000 Buddhist temples, pagodas and monasteries
were constructed in the Bagan mainly lying in the Bagan Archaeological Zone.
